How PickAd Works

PickAd allows advertisers to upload multiple versions of ad creatives, including headlines, images, banners, landing pages, and video previews. Real users participate as voters, providing authentic human feedback on which creatives perform best. This data-driven approach helps marketers make informed decisions and reduce wasted ad spend.
